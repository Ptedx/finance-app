Information We Collect

Spendr does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — transactions, categories, budgets, and settings — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device.

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

📤

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third party, for any purpose, at any time.

Spendr has no backend, no user accounts, and no cloud sync. There is nothing to share.

🔐

Permissions Used

Spendr requests only the permissions strictly necessary to provide its features:

  • Biometric authentication — optional lock screen using Face ID or fingerprint, handled entirely by the OS. No biometric data is accessed by the app.
  • Notifications — local-only reminders for recurring transactions. No notification data is sent externally.
  • Storage — read/write access to export CSV/PDF reports to your local files. Files stay on your device.
👶

Children's Privacy

Spendr does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected at all, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, the updated version will be published on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Any future features that involve data collection will be clearly disclosed before they are introduced.
